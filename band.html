Three of My Favorite Albums/CDs:

  • "Sheer Heart Attack"

    • In 1974, just three years after the group formed, "Sheer Heart Attack" was the third album the group released.
    This album was a hit in not only in the US, but in the UK as well


  • "Night At The Opera"

    • In 1975, the group released "Night At The Opera", which included the single "Bohemian Rhapsody".
    This single became one of the greatest signles of all time staying at the top of charts in the UK for
    nine weeeks. This album was a great success, leading to the band's first platinum album


  • "Greatest Hits"

    • In 1981, the band released three albums; "Greatest Hits", "Greatest Flix" and "Greatest Pix". However,
    "Greatest Hits" album was the biggest success since it has rarely been out of the UK album charts since its
    release.
